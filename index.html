FAQ
Common questions.
Who is this for?
Pre-seed founders in India preparing to pitch investors or apply to accelerators like YC, Surge, NSRCEL, or T-Hub.
How is this different from asking a mentor?
Mentors are encouraging. This is anonymous, structured, and consistent — it tells you what the investor actually thinks, not what a friend thinks.
Can I upload my pitch deck instead of filling questions?
Yes. You can either fill the structured form or upload your pitch deck as a PDF under 500KB. Both paths generate the same investor readiness report.
Will my idea be kept confidential?
Yes. Your submission is only visible to the Pre-Validation OS team. We never share or publish founder submissions.
